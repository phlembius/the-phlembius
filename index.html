WHAT IS THIS?

This is a repository to showcase my art.
Art being mostly literature works, hence why this is called the "literature repository".
Other types of art will be showcased too. Oil paintings are next on the list.
From there, I will include downloadable games. I hope to keep this free.
As this is a personal project that I will work on in my freetime, mostly at night.
So expect updates in the evening, EST.
Why this and not a web-builder? More details can be found in "ARTIST INFO" but I like doing things myself.
I also have a distain for the corporate designs web-builders use.

WHAT ART IS SHOWCASED?

Mostly writing. Poems, short plays, and short stories. In the future visual art and music may be showcased.
I like sharing art, most of the time it's with friends. Recently I've decided to share my art online.
I am not too fond of social media, and as you get to know me, this website will make a good project.
As for writing, I like more "crude" writing styles. With time I hope to learn this style of writing.
