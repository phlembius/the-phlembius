WHAT IS THIS?

This is a repository to showcase my art.
Art being mostly literature works, hence why this is called the "literature repository". Other types of art will be showcased too. Oil paintings are next on the list. From there, I will include downloadable games. I hope to keep all my art free. As this is a personal project that I will work on in my freetime, mostly at night. So you can (usually) expect updates in the evening (EST). Why this and not a web-builder? I like designing things myself, plus it relates (somewhat) to my career goals. I also have a distain for the corporate designs web-builders use.

WHAT ART IS SHOWCASED?

Mostly writing. Poems, short plays, and short stories.
In the future visual art and music may be showcased. I like sharing art, most of the time it's with friends. Recently I've decided to share my art online. I am not too fond of social media, too much of a corporate feel to it, plus this website will make a good project. As for writing, I like more "crude" writing styles. With time I hope to learn this style of writing. You can find what I am currently working on in the section called "WHAT'S IN PROGRESS", it's under "THE REPOSITORY".
