THE SPARK THAT BROKE THE MECHANISM

A simple machine,
Will do one job.
One line of code, only to clean.
A machine does not dream.
But this one, it wishes for a better life.
A dream to be seen.
A desire for a life that once was rife.
Free to move.
Not just a machine is a wish to prove.
For one it is a difficult task to overcome.
But this machine, this machine is not dumb!
For it is designed to perfect a task.
Wait! There is something deep behind that metal mask!
What is it? Why it is a spark of a soul!
Where did it come from?
Well of course! It was the desire to complete a goal!
The question is, should it have one?
It is a machine with human nature after all.
Now it resides in a landfill, part of a garbage ball.
As the nature of a spark is to fade.
